I then researched and authored an in-depth article on deepfake technology, exploring mechanisms, applications, and societal implications. Through my work I communicated complex technical concepts in an accessible manner for a wide range of non-technical audiences including students, teachers, and parents. I provided actionable recommendations for educators and parents to increase awareness and mitigate risks associated with deepfake media.
